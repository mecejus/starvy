FAQ

Frequently Asked Question

Discover fast answers to frequently asked questions about our Python course.

What is the duration of the Python course?

The course is designed to be completed in approximately 8-12 weeks, with flexible pacing to accommodate busy schedules.

Do I need prior programming experience to enroll?

No prior programming experience is required. The course is suitable for beginners and those looking to enhance their skills.

What resources are included in the course?

The course includes live group coaching sessions with a maximum of 8 participants, personally assigned programming tasks, access to our community, 24/7 chat support, links to open video resources, and recommendations for free software and AI extensions.

What kind of support is available during the course?

You will have access to 24/7 expert support, community engagement, and resources to assist you throughout your learning journey. The availability of support will depend on the coaches, who will be instantly notified of your questions and will strive to respond as quickly as possible.

Is my payment information safe?

Yes, we use Stripe for payment processing, ensuring that your payment information is secure and protected.

Are you compliant with GDPR?

Yes, we are fully GDPR compliant and do not store your email or personal information without your consent.

How can I access the course materials?

Once enrolled, you will receive an email with a link to access all course materials online at any time.

What is the pricing structure for the course?

The course operates on a monthly subscription model, allowing you to access the subscription and live coaching sessions for as long as it takes to internalize the material at your own pace. We do not offer 30-day money-back guarantee, but you can cancel your subscription at any time.
